Cyanide Spill at Baia Mare

Brief Explanation

On January 30, 2000, a tragic and devastating
cyanide spill known as the "Baia Mare Cyanide Spill" caused hundreds of fish to die. The cause of this event was a dam that burst and released a lot of water polluted with Cyanide into the Somes River, Located in Hungary and North Western Romania.

What happened?

A dam burst and let out lots of Cyanide-polluted water into a river.

Who's at fault?

The mining industry is at fault for this catastrophe.

When was this?

This occured on the 30th of January, in 2000.

Where was this?

This happened in Hungary, and North Western Romania.
